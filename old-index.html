About Me

For a natural, flawless, radiant glow — custom-made, uniquely for you.

My passion for beauty and aesthetics has been with me for many years. During my time in Los Angeles, I discovered spray tanning as a healthy alternative to sun exposure — perfect for anyone who wants a natural, radiant glow without exposing their skin to harmful UV rays.

From the very beginning, I knew that if I was going to offer spray tanning, it had to be at the highest level. That's why I trained with Dolce Glow, one of Los Angeles' most renowned spray tanning brands — whose celebrity clientele includes stars such as Kim Kardashian and Miley Cyrus.

— Betina
